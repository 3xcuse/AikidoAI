Instructors and short bios.

Sensei Hajime Tanaka discovered aikido in Kyoto at the age of six. After decades of training in Japan, he travelled across Europe sharing his knowledge.
Sensei Peter Szabo searched for a discipline that brings calm during his teenage years, which led him to aikido. He is now a founding instructor of Misogi Dojo.
Sensei Aki Yamamoto trained under renowned masters in Osaka and also practices calligraphy. He joined our dojo this year.
Sensei Anna Kovacs started her journey in judo, but her desire for harmony brought her to aikido. Today she is a role model for female practitioners.
