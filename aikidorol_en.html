About Aikido

What is Aikido?

Aikido is a traditional Japanese martial art, movement system and philosophy founded by Morihei Ueshiba in the early 20th century and later refined by his descendants and many practitioners.

It is practiced in dojos where techniques focus on receiving, avoiding and redirecting attacks, using the opponent's energy.

Aikido training also contains spiritual and philosophical aspects.
