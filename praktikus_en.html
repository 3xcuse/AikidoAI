When will you get your black belt?

With regular training and an open attitude you can reach dan level in about 6-7 years.

In our dojo 23 people earned their black belt over 22 years.

When is aikido NOT for you?

  • If you want to be the new Seagal/Bruce Lee in half a year.
  • If you seek competitions and medals.
  • If you think you improve by defeating others.
  • If you solve everything with brute force and don't want to change.
  • If you expect aikido to solve your problems instead of yourself.

Five misconceptions about Aikido

"Aikido is just dance and doesn't work in real life."

That's common among people who never practiced it.

"Moves are too complicated to learn."

"It's not a sport so it cannot be measured."

"It takes at least 10 years to learn."

"I'm too old to start."
