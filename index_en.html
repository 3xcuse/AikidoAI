What does aikido give you?

You learn to move your body in unity and improve your posture – this builds confidence and determination visible to others.

A confident person is much less likely to be bullied than someone radiating uncertainty.

You learn techniques that rely on timing, spatial awareness and spontaneous recognition rather than brute force.

The technical structure of aikido develops these skills through regular practice.

Your reaction time will shorten so you can respond before physical action fully unfolds.

Your mindset will change and you start to look at yourself and the world differently.
